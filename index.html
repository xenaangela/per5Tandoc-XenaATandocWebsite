Hi! Welcome to my website...

My name is Xena Angela Tandoc, and I’m a web design student at El Camino High School in Oceanside, California. I have a passion for creating websites that are clear, user-friendly, and visually engaging. Through studying web design, I’ve learned the importance of structure, style, and accessibility, and I’m excited to share my work here. Outside of school, I enjoy traveling, discovering new places, and of course, eating delicious food. My all-time favorite dessert is chocolate ice cream! In my free time, you’ll often find me watching Netflix and Disney+, or relaxing while listening to music.

For this main homepage, I structured the website with a header, navigation, main content area, and a footer. I added a hero section built with divs that includes a title and a short intro paragraph. I embedded CSS using link rel="stylesheet" in the head section. This embedded CSS contains the styles (fonts/colors/weight), it has three columns each linked to the "About Me", "Favorite Things, and "Contact Me" pages. I added padding on those columns, and I added margin to the left and right of my columns, as well as my texts. I have the links in the navigation section that takes it into the three pages, which is styled for hover state and the visited link in the mystyle.css page. I have a the picture above with an alt text.

Building this website has been both fun and educational, and I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed designing it. Thanks for stopping by—feel free to take a look around and let me know what you think!
